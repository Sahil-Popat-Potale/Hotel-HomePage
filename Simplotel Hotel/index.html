About Us

Situated in the heart of Jodhpur, Sahil Hotel stands as a testament to timeless elegance and heritage. Once a royal palace,

this opulent retreat has been meticulously restored to offer an unparalleled blend of traditional grandeur and modern luxury.

Guests are invited to immerse themselves in a rich history that dates back centuries, experiencing the splendor of regal architecture, exquisite décor, and bespoke service.

With lavish rooms and suites, world-class dining, and unique cultural experiences,

Sahil Hotel promises a stay that transcends mere accommodation to offer an unforgettable journey into the past,

coupled with the finest comforts of the present.

Rooms & Suites

Discover an array of luxurious accommodations at Sahil Hotel, each designed to provide the utmost comfort and elegance.

Luxurious Suites: Our spacious suites offer a perfect blend of heritage charm and modern amenities, complete with king-sized beds, plush furnishings, and private balconies with stunning views.

Heritage Rooms: Experience the grandeur of our heritage rooms, featuring intricate décor, antique furniture, and a warm, inviting atmosphere that reflects the rich history of Jodhpur.

Deluxe Rooms: Our deluxe rooms provide a cozy yet opulent retreat, with comfortable bedding, contemporary amenities, and elegant touches to ensure a restful stay.

Whether you choose a suite or a room, every accommodation option at Sahil Hotel promises an indulgent experience, ensuring a memorable stay in the heart of Jodhpur.

Fine Dining

At Sahil Hotel, we offer a variety of dining options that cater to every palate, ensuring a delightful culinary experience.

Fine Dining Restaurant: Our signature restaurant features an exquisite menu that blends traditional Indian flavors with contemporary culinary techniques, using the freshest local ingredients.

Rooftop Dining: Enjoy a romantic dinner under the stars on our rooftop terrace, with panoramic views of Jodhpur's skyline and a specially curated menu of gourmet dishes.

Specialty Cuisine: Indulge in our specialty cuisine nights, where our chefs showcase their expertise in regional and international dishes, offering a unique dining experience each time.

In-Room Dining: For those who prefer the comfort of their rooms, our in-room dining service is available 24/7, bringing a selection of our finest dishes right to your door.

Join us at Sahil Hotel and embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the rich flavors and traditions of Jodhpur, complemented by impeccable service and an inviting ambiance.

Unique Experiences

At Sahil Hotel, we strive to provide our guests with unique and unforgettable experiences that go beyond traditional hospitality.

Spa Treatments: Rejuvenate your body and mind at our luxurious spa, where we offer a range of treatments including traditional Ayurvedic massages, aromatherapy, and holistic therapies designed to promote relaxation and well-being.

Local Tours: Discover the rich history and culture of Jodhpur with our curated local tours. Visit iconic landmarks such as Mehrangarh Fort, Umaid Bhawan Palace, and explore the vibrant bazaars of the Blue City with our knowledgeable guides.

Cultural Events: Immerse yourself in the local culture with our exclusive cultural events. Enjoy live folk music and dance performances, traditional Rajasthani cuisine, and interactive workshops that give you a deeper understanding of the region's heritage.

Adventure Activities: For the adventurous spirit, we offer activities such as camel safaris, desert camping, and nature trails, allowing you to experience the rugged beauty of Rajasthan's landscapes.

Cooking Classes: Learn the art of Rajasthani cooking with our expert chefs. Participate in hands-on cooking classes where you can master traditional recipes and take home a taste of Rajasthan.

Experience the essence of Jodhpur at Sahil Hotel, where every moment is crafted to provide you with memories that last a lifetime.
